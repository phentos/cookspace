Cacio e Pepe
spaghetti, 1 lb

pecorino romano, 1 cup

black pepper, 1 tbsp

kosher salt, 1 tsp

olive oil, 3 tbsp

There are literally 1 billion ways to use spaghetti.
  1. Bring a large pot of salted water to a boil.
  2. Grate the pecorino romano.
  3. Crack the black pepper.
  4. Once the water is boiling, add the spaghetti and cook until al dente.
  5. While the spaghetti is cooking, heat the olive oil in a large skillet over medium heat.
  6. Once the oil is hot, add the black pepper and cook until fragrant.
  7. Once the spaghetti is done, reserve 1 cup of the pasta water and drain the rest.
  8. Transfer the spaghetti to the skillet and toss to coat in the oil and pepper.
  9. Remove the skillet from the heat and add the pecorino romano.
  10. Toss the spaghetti and cheese together, adding pasta water as needed to create a creamy sauce.
  11. Season with salt and more black pepper to taste.
  12. Enjoy!
